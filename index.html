Gitam Freshers Party

The Gitam Freshers Party features a grand welcome ceremony for new students, followed by lively music and dance performances that create a joyful atmosphere. The event includes fun games and ice-breaking activities to help freshers interact and build friendships. Special attractions such as the Mr. and Ms. Fresher contest add excitement and encourage participation. A photo booth captures memorable moments, while refreshments and snacks ensure everyone enjoys a pleasant and engaging evening.
